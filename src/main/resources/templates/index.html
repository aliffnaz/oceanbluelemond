About us

Welcome to D'Chalet Ombak Biru!

We are a charming chalet located in Melaka, offering a delightful retreat for travelers seeking a serene and comfortable stay.

Nestled amidst a picturesque setting, our chalet provides a peaceful atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation. With our friendly and dedicated staff, we strive to ensure your stay is memorable and enjoyable. Come and experience the tranquility of D'Chalet Ombak Biru, where comfort meets natural beauty.
